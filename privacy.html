Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Setness Consulting ("we," "us") operates the Stein application ("App"). This policy describes how we collect, use, retain, and delete information when you use the App, including when you connect a financial institution through Plaid.

1. Information we collect

Through Plaid and related services, the App may process:

  • Account identifiers, names, and masks;
  • Transaction history, balances, and related financial metadata;
  • Technical data needed to maintain the connection (for example, item identifiers and token metadata);
  • Operational logs for troubleshooting (logs do not include full access tokens).

2. How we use information

We use information only to:

  • Provide reconciliation, audit feeds, and financial analysis you request;
  • Maintain security, reliability, and troubleshooting;
  • Meet legal and recordkeeping obligations where applicable.

We do not sell your personal data.

3. Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties except: Plaid and your financial institution as required to provide connectivity; service providers that help run the App under confidentiality obligations; or when required by law.

4. Retention and deletion

Plaid access tokens are kept only while the connection is active and deleted within 30 days after disconnect. Transaction data is retained up to seven years where needed for tax or recordkeeping, or deleted sooner when no longer needed. You may request deletion by contacting us below.

5. Security

We use reasonable safeguards including encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+), encryption at rest on secured devices, least-privilege access, and multi-factor authentication on administrative systems.

6. Your choices

You can disconnect the App from your financial institution through Plaid or by contacting us. Revocation may disable App functionality that depends on that connection.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will change when we do. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
